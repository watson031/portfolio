Waiting List

A mobile application that allows you to make an appointment at a garage, to know exactly the time remaining before our turn and to receive notifications update about the time remaining. The mechanic has an interface allowing him to mention when he is free or when he has finished with a customer. Changes made in real time by the mechanic affect the waiting time.

Technologies used : Java, Android, Node Js, Express, Heroku, PostgreSQL
